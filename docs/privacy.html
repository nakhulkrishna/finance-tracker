Policy

What information Finance Tracker uses.

Account information

Finance Tracker may store account details such as name, email address, and profile preferences to support sign in and a personalized app experience.

Finance records

The app stores finance data such as transactions, wallet entries, investment entries, opening balances, categories, and related settings so users can use the core product features.

Cloud storage

Finance Tracker uses Firebase services for authentication and cloud-backed app data storage so signed-in users can access their finance records more reliably.

Premium purchases

Premium purchases are handled through Apple In-App Purchase. The app may store mirrored premium status information to reflect access to premium features inside the app.
